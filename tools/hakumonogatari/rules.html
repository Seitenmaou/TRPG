Story

Ninty-nine stories told. Exists a single story left. The narrative remains incomplete. A story before hundred. Ignorant of what hides in stories already told.

You are amongst people, each bringing a ghost story. 99 have been told already. With the 100th one told, there shall be a disaster upon the world. You must prevent the 100th ghost story from being completed.

Gameplay and Rules

If you want the summary, here it is: make the story not scary

    If you want the detailed one:
  • There are players (PL) and game master(GM).
  • GM will be reading the story, and their objective is to eliminate every PL.
  • Each PL has a class and hit points, and their objective is to guess all keywords in the story.
  • The story progression as follows:
    • Before the the beggning, GM prepares a story along with keywords (usually 10 keywords).
    • At game start, the PLs will pick a class with set hit points and abilities, along with a number from 1~6.
    • Then the game really begins, GM will read the story, and the PLs (any of the PL, there is no order to follow) will object to a word or phrase
    • An example would be:
      • GM: So yesterday I was walking down the street to my school
      • Pl1: wait no, i thought it was jogging , not walking?
    • Then the player would roll 1d6, or a 6 face dice. As long as they dont land on the number they chose at the beggning, they succeed changing the phrase, otherwise they lose a life point
    • Following the previous example
      • If Pl1 had chosen the number 3 at the beggning, and he rolled a 3, the word "walking" would not change, and he would lose one life point
      • If PL1 has rolled anything else, EVERY occurence of the word "walk/walking" would change to "jog/jogging" (feel free to be picky or forgiving on words' tense)
      • If PL1 had rolled 3, and that was his last life point, they are eliminated. You can no longer change phrases. (there are exceptions related to class abilities, noted later)
      • If the altered word or phrase containes any of the ten keywords, the GM should announce it on the first occurnece. (due note, they do not have to announce the total or remaining amount of keywords)
    • This continues until the end of the story, regardless of remaing keywords or players. (its more fun that way!)
    • Once the story is over, check the win condition
      • GM wins if all players have reached zero hit points
      • PL wins if all keywords have been found. (AND altered!)
      • If even one PL is alive and even one keyword was not found, the GM will take a vote by asking if the story remained scary at the end. If yes, GM wins, if no, its a Tie.
  • Usually takes 1 hour for a single session
  • Lastly, this is a "game" but its not too much about win or losing, it has a huge emphasis on having fun!

Classes

    Feel free to adjust HP as needed (EX: Less players, more HP per)
  • Screen Writer [Starting HP: 3] : can change phrases even if you fail the dice check (you still take damage)
  • Psychic [Starting HP: 4] : can take damage in place of someone
  • Editor [Starting HP: 3] : when you take damage, the GM must reveal a new keyword
  • Scientist [Starting HP: 2] : You can change two words at a time per dice roll
  • Shaman [Starting HP: 6] : On successful dice roll, one player with the number that was rolled takes damage
  • Neet [Starting HP: 5] : nothing special, people get to make fun of you, you neet
